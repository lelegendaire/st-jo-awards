# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Students, parents, and attendees of the Saint-Joseph school awards ceremony. They access the voting interface by scanning a QR code displayed at the event, using their phones in a dimly lit venue with stage lighting. The mood is excitement and anticipation — they're participating in a live ceremony.

## Product Purpose

Transform a simple voting mechanism into an immersive digital extension of the awards ceremony. Every interaction should feel like part of the show: cinematic, premium, and memorable. Success means participants feel they're voting from the audience of a real awards show, not filling out a form.

## Brand Personality

Luxurious, theatrical, electric. Voice: confident and ceremonial. Tone: elevated but not pretentious. Emotional goals: excitement, anticipation, pride in participation, the thrill of being part of something special.

## Anti-references

- Generic survey tools (Google Forms, SurveyMonkey aesthetics)
- Flat SaaS dashboards
- Children's party templates
- Corporate training portals
- Anything that looks like a form

## Design Principles

1. **The screen is a stage.** Every element is part of the performance. Nothing is decorative without purpose.
2. **Motion tells the story.** Transitions are cinematic, not mechanical. Each answer click is a beat in the show.
3. **Gold is the spotlight.** Use gold sparingly and deliberately — it should feel like a spotlight hitting the right moment.
4. **Darkness is the canvas.** The deep black background isn't absence of light — it's the darkness of a theater before the curtain rises.
5. **One moment at a time.** The voting flow is sequential and focused. Never overwhelm with multiple questions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Respect prefers-reduced-motion: provide non-animated fallbacks
- Maintain sufficient contrast between gold text and dark backgrounds
- Touch targets must be large enough for mobile use in low light
- Screen reader support for all interactive elements
